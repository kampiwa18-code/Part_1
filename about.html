About The Daily Grind Café

Founded in 2023 in Cape Town, South Africa, The Daily Grind Café is a welcoming local neighborhood coffee shop. Over the last three years, it has transformed into a vibrant community hub and comfortable workspace tailored for local students, employees and neighborhood residents looking for authentic human connections.

Our café focuses exclusively on serving premium espresso-based beverages and fresh house-baked goods. Our establishment strictly operates as a beverage and food venue. We do not inventory, pack, or distribute retail bags of whole or ground coffee beans.

Guided by an eco-conscious ethos, we prioritize transparent local ingredient sourcing practices with a clear mission to brighten our Western Cape communitys day one handcrafted cup at a time.
