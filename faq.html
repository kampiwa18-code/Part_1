Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have free Wi-Fi and power outlets?

Yes! We offer high-speed, uncapped Wi-Fi and easily accessible power outlets for remote workers, students, and professionals.

Are there vegan or gluten-free options on the menu?

Absolutely. We offer a variety of dairy alternatives (oat, almond, and soy), as well as vegan and gluten-free pastries, sandwiches, and warm breakfast bowls.

Can I reserve a table in advance?

Yes, you can! If you are planning a small meeting or catching up with a large group, please Contact us to secure your table.

Are pets allowed in the cafe?

We love furry friends! Well-behaved, leashed dogs are welcome in our outdoor seating area, where we always have fresh water bowls ready.

Do you use locally sourced coffee beans?

We certainly do. All our espresso and filter brews feature 100% ethically sourced, premium African coffee beans.
